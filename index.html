I have lived in New Jersey my whole life so it is safe to say I am a Jersey boy at heart. To my family and friends' surprise, I took a leap into travel medicine and am enjoying the new adventures.

I created this blog to share my experiences as a travel med tech and shed some light on this mysterious profession. In doing so, I hope to be a resource for those new and experienced to the travel healthcare profession.

The Nomad Scientist serves as a place where we all can connect with others for our love of travel and medicine (and money). Thank you for joining me on my nomad journey…

My First Assignment!

Geisinger Medical Center
Danville, Pennyslvania

The lab at GMC comes with great technology and more importantly, great people. Training is comprehensive for both laboratory LIS and department specific processes. There is a constant influx of travelers making this a traveler friendly facility. Would I recommend this lab to any future laboratory travelers? Yes, absolutely yes! Or, as the good folk from central valley PA say, “Yippersssss!”

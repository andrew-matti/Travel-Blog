The Nomad Scientist

Hi! I'm Andrew.

I began my career as a Medical Laboratory Scientist in 2017 and worked at various settings within Atlantic Health System.

Living in New Jersey for my entire life, it is safe to say I am a Jersey boy at heart. So to my family and friends' surprise, in September 2021, I took a leap into travel medicine. Since then, I have not looked back.

I created this blog to share my experiences as a travel lab scientist. Perhaps, this can shed some light on this mysterious profession. My hope is to inspire those interested in becoming a travel lab scientist; or even, to open the door into a career as a Medical Laboratory Science.

The Nomad Scientist serves as a place where we all can connect with others for our love of travel and medicine (and money).
